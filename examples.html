Part One

Building the Character

Elizabeth Abbott is the squire of the villain Lord Drulex. She lives in the realm of Otherworlds with her adoptive father, Rome Shanaam, and is secretly being trained by Drulex, who sends her to the realm of Lyra Sii to infiltrate Hamrock, School of Keepers, by becoming a student and help Drulex steal the Stone of Bularas, which will free all the criminals from the exile realm of Bularas.

Initial Conflict

Once Elizabeth is in the school, her goal becomes to win the favor of the professors to ensure her spot as a torch-bearer in the annual Realm Wars Rememberance Ceremony in which the Stone of Bularas is brought out once a year to free one prisoner for the Hour of Jubilee. To do so, she decides to compete in the upcoming student competitions.

Proving Character's Worth

Elizabeth's team of three included herself and two boys her age named Vrik and Cobi. During the training for the games, she has to overcome her tendency to be a lone player and learn to work with a team.
